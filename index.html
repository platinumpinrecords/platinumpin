About the Label

A small label with uncompromising standards for the artists it signs.

Platinum Pin Records is an independent label focused on developing distinctive voices in modern music. Founded with a long-term view rather than a quick-hit mentality, the imprint operates on the principle that great records take time, attention, and a team that actually listens.

The label handles distribution, brand development, and release strategy for its roster — currently led by flagship artist b3lac. Every release is treated as an artifact: considered packaging, considered timing, considered audience.
